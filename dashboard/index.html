Confirmation is disabled

A live detail view must display a decision ID, redacted target and parameters, reason, expiry, context IDs, payload-hash fingerprint, routing class, and execution warning. It must resubmit the displayed ID and hash only. Stale, mismatched, expired, and execution-in-progress requests remain unapproved.

Devices

Device posture

No device list is cached in the browser. Future records use opaque identities and distinguish local revocation from broker completion or pending reconciliation.

Devices unavailable

When connected, each record may show exact capabilities, enrollment and ACL versions, transport, and last report. Capture is reported as unknown or last-reported only; an absent report is never shown as “off”.

Timeline & retention

Derived-only records

This screen will never offer playback, download, raw media, media paths, credentials, recovery material, or unredacted action parameters.

Timeline unavailable

Future rows are limited to time, opaque device identity, modality, classification, summary, retention/expiry or pin status, and opaque media-reference presence. Expired records are absent by default.

Audit

Append-only accountability

Audit history will be newest-first, redacted, permanent, non-editable, and non-deletable. This static screen contains no cached audit events.

Audit unavailable

Policy denials, conflicts, expired decisions, and pending broker reconciliation will be clearly represented once the authenticated API exists.
